    Exploration and Exploitation

    Should I be sticking to it, pivoting, or moving on? That's the question relevant at any point in life.

    When things go well, think of exploitation versus exploration. Often times, it is better off keeping the position (exploitation). Every now and then, you can try something new (exploitation) to see if you are missing out anything.

    When things don't go well, think of tenacity versus flexibility. Should I stick to it until things turn around (tenacity)? Or should I pivot or even quit (flexibility)? While a rolling stone gathers no moss, don't get trapped in sunk cost fallacy.
